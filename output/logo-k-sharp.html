Musk — sharp-angle uppercase K

Straight strokes, sharp corners (no rounding at the fork). Flow + power come from tapered/streamline ends and direction, not curves. Pick one (S1–S4).

S1 · Miter
A clean sharp K: straight strokes meeting at a mitered (pointed) fork. Round caps only at the very tips give a tapered streamline end; the corner itself stays sharp.
sharp · clean
S2 · Taper-tail
Sharp mitered K whose two prong tips carry thin trailing tails — streamline motion-blur. Power reads at the sharp corner, flow reads at the tapered exits.
sharp · streamline
S3 · Asym-sharp
Sharp straight K with unequal prong lengths and different angles — short steep upper, long shallow lower. Reads as a directed thrust, all corners sharp.
sharp · directed
S4 · App-icon
The sharp mitered K on a rounded gradient tile with white strokes — a real app-icon / favicon that scales cleanly.
