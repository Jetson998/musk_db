Musk — balanced fluid-power K

Proportioned like a real letter K (symmetric prongs, fork around the vertical middle), with streamlined tapered ends and a touch of curve. Pick one (B1–B4).

B1 · Taper
A properly proportioned K: symmetric prongs of equal length and angle, forking at the stem's middle. Round caps give each end a streamlined taper; a faint tail on the stem adds flow without breaking the letter.
balanced · tapered
B2 · Bow
Same balanced symmetric prongs, but the stem carries a gentle curve — a bow under tension. Power lives in the stem, flow lives in the curve.
tension · balanced
B3 · Swept-tip
Symmetric equal prongs that go straight, then sweep outward right at the tips — force exiting through the ends. Balanced letter, fluid exits.
balanced · swept
B4 · Ribbon
Symmetric equal prongs, each flowing out then curling into a small tapered tail at the end — a continuous ribbon. Most fluid while staying a balanced K.
balanced · ribbon
