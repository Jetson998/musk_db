Musk — uppercase K, curved

A normal capital K (one-sided, stem + two prongs on the right). The prongs are curved, not straight — giving the fluid + power feel without breaking the letter. Pick one (C1–C4).

C1 · Bow-out
A normal K whose two prongs bow outward in a gentle arc before reaching their tips. Symmetric, clean — the curve gives flow, the stem gives structure.
