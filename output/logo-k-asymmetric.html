Musk — asymmetric fluid-power K

Upper and lower prongs differ in angle AND length. Stays simple (3 strokes), keeps the fluid + power feel. Pick one (A1–A4).

A1 · Thrust
Short steep upper prong, long shallow lower prong that sweeps far out. Reads as a downward/forward thrust — force directed.
thrust · long-low
A2 · Lift
Long sweeping upper prong, short steep lower prong. Mirror of A1 — reads as lift/launch upward.
lift · long-high
A3 · Drift
Curved bow stem + asymmetric swept prongs (short steep up with tail, long shallow down with tail). Most fluid while asymmetric.
flow · asymmetric
A4 · Mix
Upper prong long + curved (fluid), lower prong short + straight (mechanical). Mixed character — flow meets a hard stop.
fluid × mechanical
