Musk — concise fluid-power K

Simple. Flow + power. 4 marks, each is basically a K drawn as one or two confident strokes with tapered/streamlined ends. Pick one (P1–P4).

P1 · Taper
The plain K1 form, but every stroke uses a round cap so each end reads as a tapered streamline. Minimal, no extra parts.
clean · flow
P2 · Bow
Stem carries a gentle curve — like a drawn bow under tension. Same three confident strokes, just with stored energy.
tension · power
P3 · Sweep
Prongs bend then sweep outward at the ends — like force expelled through a nozzle. Flow direction is legible.
flow · expelled
P4 · Ribbon
Prongs start from the stem and curl into little tapered tails at the ends — a continuous ribbon of flow. Most "fluid" while staying just three strokes.
ribbon · fluid
